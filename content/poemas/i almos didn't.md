---
title: i almost didn't notice
author: sara rian
type: verso
---

i almost didn’t notice
how december writes the eulogy
and leads us in the year’s farewell.​
or how the moon sits in the sky waiting
to guide us through the death of every day.​
or how the shore prays as the tide retreats.​
how did it took so long to finally see​
the quiet funerals all around me.
